Welcome to the 2021 Relish Food & Wine Festival

Enjoy local food, regional wines, beers and fabulous music in the Mary River Parklands.

On Saturday 5 June 2021, the annual Relish Food & Wine Festival will return with delicious events, great food, local wine and live music. Stayed tuned closer to the date for a program that will showcase the very best the Fraser Coast has to offer – whether you’re looking to wine and dine, wander the heritage streetscapes tasting what’s on offer or groove to some soulful tunes, Relish Food & Wine Festival has something for you.
