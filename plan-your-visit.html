Visiting to the Fraser Coast?

Frequently asked questions

Where Can I buy tickets?

You can buy tickets here or visit the Maryborough or Hervey Bay Visitor Information Centres.

Can I BYO alcohol?

Relish is a licensed venue, there is no BYO alcohol.

Can I BYO Chairs?

Yes, as seating is limited we encourage people to bring chairs and picnic rugs.

What food is available?

There is a wide range of hot food vendors plus a range of specialty stores.

Can I bring my pet?

Support animals only.

I’ve bought a session ticket does this include entry to the festival?

To gain entry to the festival you will need to purchase a general admission ticket separately. Please note tickets on the day will increase.

Are there any road closures?

Yes, The following roads will be closed from 12pm on Friday the 4th of June to 12pm Monday the 7th of June 2021. - Richmond Street (between Kent Street and Wharf Street). The following roads will be closed from 12pm Friday the 4th of June to 12pm Sunday the 6th of June 2021: - Wharf Street (between Bazaar Street and March Street); and - Richmond Street (between Kent Street and the Excelsior Band Hall Car Park).

Are there toilets on site?

Yes we have toilets including disabled facilities.

Is there an ATM on site?

There’s an ATM available at Cozii Café.

Can I pay with card?

Card facilities are available at most stalls. Card payments are preferred at the Craft Beer Bar & Ticket Gates..

Can I buy session tickets on the day?

Session tickets on the day are subject to availability and can be purchased from the ticket booth outside the main gate. It is recommended to purchase online to avoid disappointment.
